# Square One Decor - Client Information & Content Requirement Checklist

## Purpose

This document lists all information, assets, credentials, and content required from the client to build a complete, professional, SEO-friendly, responsive, and production-ready company website.

---

# 1. Company Information

## Required Data

- Company Name
- Tagline / Slogan
- Company Logo (PNG, SVG, AI)
- Company Introduction
- Company History
- Vision Statement
- Mission Statement
- Core Values

## Used In

- Navbar
- Hero Section
- About Section
- Footer
- SEO Meta Tags
- Open Graph Data

---

# 2. Brand Identity

## Required Data

- Typography Preferences
- Brand Guidelines (if available)
- Marketing Brochure
- Company Profile PDF

- Typography System
- Dark/Light Themes
